PC Rules and Policies

Normal PCs (Standard Public PCs):

1. Time Limits: Users are allowed to use Normal PCs for up to 2 hours per session. Extensions may be available based on availability.
2. Respect Others: Maintain respectful behavior toward other users. No harassment, foul language, or disruptive conduct.
3. No Food or Drink:To maintain cleanliness and prevent damage to equipment, no food or drinks are allowed near the PCs.
4. Installation Restrictions: Do not install unauthorized software or modify system settings. Only games and apps from the approved list can be accessed.
5. Data Storage: Save your work or game progress to external storage (USB or cloud storage) before your session ends.
6. Personal Use: Computers are to be used for gaming and browsing; avoid inappropriate or illegal content.
7. Damage or Malfunctions: Immediately report any hardware or software issues to staff. Any damage caused by misuse may incur a charge.

VIP PCs (Premium Access):

1. Usage Time Limit:Users are allowed a set amount of time (6 hour) per session to ensure fairness for all users. Extensions may be granted if no other users are waiting.
2. Software Usage:Only authorized software should be used. Installation of unauthorized software is prohibited.
3. Internet Usage:No illegal or disruptive activities, including downloading illegal content, excessive streaming, or accessing inappropriate websites.
4. Food & Drinks: No food or drinks near the PCs to prevent spills and damage to equipment.
5. Device Privacy: Do not tamper with other users' work, and do not leave personal information unattended. Users should sign out of their accounts when finished.
6. Noise Level: Keep noise to a minimum to respect other users.
7. Cleanliness: Users are expected to leave their stations clean and tidy. Dispose of trash in appropriate bins.
8. Behavior: Maintain respectful and courteous behavior towards others. No harassment or disruptive conduct will be tolerated.

Streaming Room

1. Booking: The streaming room must be booked in advance. No walk-in usage is allowed without prior reservation.
2. Time Limit:Sessions in the streaming room are typically limited to a set amount of time (e.g., 2 hours) to allow access for other users.
3. Content Creation:All content must comply with legal, ethical, and platform-specific guidelines. No inappropriate or illegal content is allowed.
4. Equipment Handling: All equipment (cameras, microphones, lights, etc.) must be used responsibly. Do not alter or break equipment. Users are responsible for returning items in the condition they were found.
5. Noise Level: Keep noise to a minimum during use to avoid disturbing others in nearby areas.
6. Setup and Clean-up: Users must set up their own equipment and clean up afterward, ensuring the room is left in a usable condition for the next person.
7. Food & Drinks: No food or drinks are allowed in the streaming room to protect equipment from spills and damage.
8. Broadcasting Rules: Ensure that all live streams and recordings comply with applicable terms of service for platforms (e.g., Twitch, YouTube). Avoid sharing personal information or violating any platform guidelines.
9. Professional Conduct: Streaming and recording should be done in a professional manner. Disruptive or inappropriate behavior in the streaming room may result in penalties or loss of access.
